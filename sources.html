Our Standards

How We Use Sources

  • Every factual claim is linked to a verifiable source
  • We prefer primary sources (government data, official reports) over secondary
  • When sources conflict, we note the discrepancy
  • We distinguish between facts and our interpretation
  • If we make an error, we correct it publicly
